Most algo trading sellers show you their best weeks. Their equity curves start in the bottom left and end in the top right — no drawdowns, no flat periods, no ugly months.

We don't do that.

**We show every trade. Every loss. Every drawdown.**

Not because we're masochists, but because that's the only way to know if a strategy actually works.

## The problem with cherry-picked results

A strategy that makes money during a bull run isn't necessarily edge. It might just be beta. The only way to know is to see the full distribution — the wins *and* the losses.

When you see a flat month on our dashboard, that's real. When you see a drawdown, that's real. And when you see the recovery, that's real too.

## What we're building

AlgoProof is a platform where we publish the full trading history of our APEX algo trading bots. Right now we're in paper trading — simulated execution to validate strategies before risking real capital.

Our bots run EMA crossover strategies on crypto (Binance, Hyperliquid) with a 4-layer risk defense system and Market Intelligence filtering.

When the data proves the strategy works over 2+ months of paper trading, we go live. And you'll see that transition happen here, in real time.
